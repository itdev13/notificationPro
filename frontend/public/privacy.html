Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Our Commitment: NotifyPro is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and security.

1. Information We Collect

Information from Your CRM

When you install NotifyPro, we collect and process:

  • Account Information: Location ID, Company ID, User ID
  • Conversation Data: New conversation notifications including contact name, message content, and timestamps
  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens to communicate with your CRM API
  • Webhook Data: Real-time events from your CRM for notification triggers

Information You Provide

  • Notification Preferences: Email address, Slack webhook URL, business hours
  • Filter Settings: Priority keywords, custom notification rules
  • Push Subscription: Browser push notification subscription data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide notification services:

  • Deliver Notifications: Send real-time alerts via browser, email, and Slack
  • Filter & Process: Apply your custom rules and business hours to notifications
  • Maintain Service: Store OAuth tokens securely for API communication
  • Improve Experience: Analyze notification patterns to enhance the service
Important: We NEVER sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Data

  • All data is stored on secure AWS servers with encryption at rest
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • Database access is restricted and monitored

Security Measures

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Automatic token refresh and expiration handling

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data stored while your account is active
  • Notification History: Kept for 30 days for troubleshooting
  • OAuth Tokens: Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Account Deletion: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of uninstall

5. Third-Party Services

NotifyPro integrates with the following services:

  • CRM Platform: For conversation data and authentication
  • Email Service: For sending email notifications (via AWS SES)
  • Slack: Only if you provide a Slack webhook URL
  • Push Services: Browser-native push notification services

Each service has its own privacy policy. We share only the minimum data required for functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: Update your notification preferences
  • Deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all your data
  • Export: Request an export of your settings and configuration
  • Opt-out: Disable specific notification channels anytime

7. Cookies and Tracking

NotifyPro uses minimal cookies:

  • Authentication: Session cookies for user authentication
  • Preferences: Local storage for notification settings
  • Analytics: Basic usage analytics (anonymized)

We do NOT use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

NotifyPro is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • In-app notification in NotifyPro settings
  • Update notice on this page with the new "Last Updated" date

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@notifypro.app
  • Support: Use the in-app support tab
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
GDPR & CCPA Compliance: NotifyPro is designed to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. We are committed to protecting your rights and handling your data responsibly.
