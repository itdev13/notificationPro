‚ö° Quick Start Guide

Follow these simple steps to start receiving real-time notifications:

Install from GHL Marketplace

Navigate to the GoHighLevel Marketplace and search for "NotifyPro". Click "Install" and authorize the app to access your location.

Configure OAuth Permissions

Grant NotifyPro permission to:

  • Read conversations
  • Access contact information
  • Receive webhook notifications
Note: These permissions are required for NotifyPro to send you real-time alerts.

Access NotifyPro Settings

After installation, find "NotifyPro" in your sub-account's left sidebar. Click to open the settings dashboard.

Enable Notification Channels

Choose your preferred notification methods:

  • Browser Push: Click "Enable Push Notifications" (allow browser permission)
  • Email: Enter your email address
  • Slack: Provide your Slack webhook URL (optional)

Set Business Hours (Optional)

Configure when you want to receive notifications:

  • Set start and end times
  • Choose active days of the week
  • Select your timezone

Add Priority Keywords (Optional)

Define important keywords for special alerts (e.g., "urgent", "refund", "cancel"). Messages containing these keywords will get priority notifications.

Test Your Notifications

Click "Send Test Notification" to verify everything is working correctly. You should receive a test alert on all enabled channels.

‚úì Setup Complete! You're now ready to receive real-time notifications for new GoHighLevel conversations.

üîî Browser Push Notifications

üìß Email Notifications

Get detailed email alerts with conversation context and contact information.

Setup:
  1. Enter your email address in settings
  2. Click "Verify Email" to confirm
  3. Check spam folder if verification email doesn't arrive

Email Contains: Contact name, phone/email, message preview, timestamp, direct link to conversation

üí¨ Slack Integration

Forward notifications to your Slack workspace for team collaboration.

Setup:
  1. Create a Slack Incoming Webhook in your workspace
  2. Copy the webhook URL
  3. Paste it into NotifyPro settings
  4. Choose your notification channel
Getting a Slack Webhook URL:
  1. Go to https://api.slack.com/apps
  2. Create a new app or select existing app
  3. Enable "Incoming Webhooks"
  4. Add webhook to your workspace
  5. Copy the webhook URL

üîß Troubleshooting

Not Receiving Browser Notifications?

Common Solutions:
  • Check browser notification permissions (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Notifications)
  • Make sure Do Not Disturb is off (macOS/Windows)
  • Try re-enabling push notifications in NotifyPro settings
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Disable browser extensions that block notifications

Email Notifications Not Arriving?

Check These:
  • Verify email address in settings
  • Check spam/junk folder
  • Add notifications@notifypro.app to contacts
  • Confirm email settings are saved
  • Test with "Send Test Notification" button

Slack Notifications Not Working?

Verify:
  • Webhook URL is correct and active
  • Slack app has necessary permissions
  • Webhook hasn't been revoked in Slack
  • Check Slack channel settings
  • Test webhook with curl command first

OAuth Errors?

Try:
  • Uninstall and reinstall NotifyPro
  • Clear browser cache
  • Log out and back into GoHighLevel
  • Contact support if issue persists

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How many notifications can I receive?

Unlimited! NotifyPro has no limits on notification volume. However, we do implement rate limiting to prevent abuse.

Can I use NotifyPro on multiple locations?

Yes! Each GoHighLevel location requires a separate NotifyPro subscription. Settings are managed per location.

What happens during my free trial?

You get full access to all features for 3 days. No credit card required. After the trial, it's $30/month per location.

Can I customize notification sounds?

Browser notification sounds are controlled by your operating system settings. Priority notifications use a different system sound.

Does NotifyPro work on mobile?

Browser push notifications work on Android Chrome/Firefox. iOS Safari has limited support. Email and Slack notifications work everywhere.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Uninstall NotifyPro from the GoHighLevel Marketplace. Your subscription will automatically cancel.
